Benefits Guide

TechCorp offers a comprehensive benefits package to support employee well-being.

Health Insurance

- Medical: PPO plan with low deductibles
- Vision: Annual eye exams covered
- Prescription drugs: Generic and brand name options

Dental Insurance

- Provider: Delta Dental
- Coverage: Preventive (100%), Basic (80%), Major (50%)
- Includes cleanings, fillings, crowns, and orthodontics for dependents
- Annual maximum: $2,000 per person

Retirement Plans

- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Roth IRA options available

Paid Time Off

- Vacation: 15 days per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Holidays: 10 company holidays

For enrollment or questions, visit the benefits portal or contact HR.
