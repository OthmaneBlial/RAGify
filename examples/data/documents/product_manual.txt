Product Manual - SmartHome Hub

Overview

The SmartHome Hub connects all your smart devices for seamless control.

Features
- Voice control with Alexa integration
- App-based management
- Energy monitoring

Setup
1. Plug in the hub
2. Download the app
3. Follow on-screen instructions

Troubleshooting
- If device not responding: Restart hub and check Wi-Fi
- App issues: Clear cache or reinstall app

Password Reset
- Go to account settings in app
- Select 'Forgot Password'
- Follow email instructions
