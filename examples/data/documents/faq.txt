Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I troubleshoot my device?
A: Check connections, restart device, update firmware via app.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Use 'Forgot Password' in the login screen, check email for reset link.

Q: What is the return policy?
A: 30-day return for unused items in original packaging. Contact support for RMA.

Q: Device not connecting to Wi-Fi?
A: Ensure 2.4GHz network, check password, move closer to router.

Q: How to update firmware?
A: Open app, go to device settings, select 'Check for Updates'.

Q: Warranty information?
A: 1-year limited warranty covers defects. Contact support for claims.
