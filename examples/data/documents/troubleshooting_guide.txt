Troubleshooting Guide

Common Issues and Solutions

1. Device Not Powering On
- Check power adapter and outlet
- Try different cable
- Reset by holding power button for 10 seconds

2. Wi-Fi Connection Problems
- Verify network credentials
- Ensure device is within range
- Restart router and device

3. App Not Responding
- Force close and reopen app
- Clear app cache
- Update to latest version

4. Voice Commands Not Working
- Check microphone permissions
- Ensure internet connection
- Re-link voice assistant

5. Firmware Update Failed
- Ensure stable internet
- Restart device during update
- Contact support if persists

6. Password Reset Issues
- Check spam folder for reset email
- Use correct email address
- Contact support for manual reset

If issues persist, gather device info and contact support@smarthome.com.
