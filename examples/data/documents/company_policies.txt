Company Policies

Code of Conduct

All employees must maintain professional behavior, respect colleagues, and comply with laws.

Remote Work Policy

- Eligible for remote work: Full-time employees after 3 months
- Frequency: Up to 3 days per week
- Equipment: Company provides laptop and monitors
- Security: Use VPN for company data

Expense Reports

- Submit via online system
- Categories: Travel, meals, supplies
- Limits: Meals up to $50/day, travel pre-approved
- Deadlines: Within 30 days

Confidentiality

Do not share proprietary information. Use secure channels.

Disciplinary Actions

Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or termination.

Contact HR for policy clarifications.
